METHONTOLOGY + MOMo

An OWL 2 ontology for representing movies, TV series, episodes, persons (actors, directors, writers, producers, composers), genres, awards, production companies, countries, languages, franchises, and streaming platforms. Designed for an IMDb-like knowledge graph supporting SPARQL querying, Wikidata-based population, and LLM-driven enrichment.
